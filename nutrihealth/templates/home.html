What we do



Help make health tracking easier

We strive for an effortless approach to health tracking using only the necessary tools to get started. Make a dedicated meal and exercise plan, search for meals, and connect with friends in our simple and manageable dashboard.



Learn what's in your food

Knowing the nutrition information in our food is vital in establishing a healthy meal plan. Learn about nutrition information, serving size, and calorie count while using our food database.



Help you stay motivated

We believe staying motivated with health goals starts with having a accountability partner. Clients can interact with friends in our friends tracker feature to encourage themselves and others to stay focused on their health goals.
